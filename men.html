Online: Mixed Issues (Men)

This online group is for Men struggling in any area. You are encouraged to participate in this group if you are joining us remotely on any given week. If you are a newcomer, we encourage you to schedule a newcomers 101 orientation prior to joining this group.

In Person Groups (Men)

Addictive Behaviors:
If you find that you cannot quit drinking or using entirely, even when you honestly want to, or if you have little control over the amount you consume, you'll find support in this group.
More Info on Chemical Dependency


Sexual Integrity:
Support group for those seeking recovery from lust and compulsive sexual behaviors. This group is a safe place to share our struggles, pain, and victories, and find accountability and mutual support among the group members.
More Info on Sexual Integrity


Anger, Anxiety, & Life Issues
This group is for those struggling with anger, anxiety, the compulsion to rescue and take care of others, have difficulty setting boundaries, or recognizing their own worth. Members in this group learn to express their own needs and wants in healthy ways. Other struggling in an area not specified can find a home in this group also.
