Women - In Person: Sexual Integrity
Support group for those seeking recovery from lust and compulsive sexual behaviors. This group is a safe place to share our struggles, pain, and victories, and find accountability and mutual support among the group members throughout the week.
More Info
Women - Online: COSA & SPEVA
This group combines 2 smaller groups: COSA, an acronym for COdependent in a relationship with a Sexually Addicted man, and SPEVA, that stans for Sexual, Physical, Emotional, & Verbal Abuse. This group offers support towards healing from the traumas done to us at sometime in our past, as well as healing from the influence these past experiences continue to have on our lives.

Women - Online: Codependency & Mixed Issues
This group is for those struggling with the compulsion to rescue and take care of others, have difficulty setting boundaries, or recognizing their own worth. Members in this group learn to express their own needs and wants in healthy ways. Other struggling in an area not specified can find a home in this group also.
