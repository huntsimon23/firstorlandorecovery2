Step Studies

CR Step Studies with First Baptist Orlando CR (FBOCR) meet at various times and locations. Step Studies launch when we have 2 leaders, a group of 10 or more ready to start, and agreement on day/time/location. During COVID-19, they may also be available online. Step Studies are gender specific - men with men, women with women. They are NOT issue specific - all areas of recovery in CR participate together.

By completing this form, you are expressing your interest in joining the next available step study and also being notified by email about future step studies thru FBOCR.
