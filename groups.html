Open Share Groups

  • All Groups meet from 7:45pm to 8:30pm on Tuesdays.
  • All Groups are gender specific - men with men, women with women.
  • Please note the location: some groups are in person (Language Ministry Center), some online (Zoom Link)
  • Online groups require a device capable of operating the zoom application, utilizing both webcam and headphones.
  • For those who wish to attend large group in person but attend an online group, there will be rooms set aside for Men and for Women to do that - but each participant MUST still use their own device to participate. No sharing devices please.
