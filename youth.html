FAQ's For Parents:

  • Are there any costs for childcare?
      All programs are offered at no cost at CR! However we do operate on a donation basis and a gift of any amount helps us to keep the program running.
    Donate to FBOCR
  • Where do I go on Tuesday when I get to the Language Ministry Center?
    KIDS Programming will be located in the back hallway.  Please enter from the RIGHT side, down the hallway past the offices.
  • Does my child need to wear a mask?
      All staff and volunteers will be wearing masks. We have masks available at our check in desks for kids who would like to wear one, however, masks are not required for children attending KIDS programming.
  • We’ll all need to work together to help keep KIDS safe, so please stay home and participate in CR Online if your child:
    Has had a fever in the past 14 days.
    Shows any symptoms of COVID-19.
    Has been around anyone who has tested positive for COVID-19 in the past 14 days.
